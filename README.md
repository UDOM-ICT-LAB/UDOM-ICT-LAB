# UDOM ICT LAB

**UDOM ICT LAB** is an innovation-driven ICT and open-source laboratory at the **University of Dodoma (UDOM)**.  
The lab brings together students, researchers and developers to design, build and share practical digital solutions that address real-world challenges in Tanzania and beyond.
